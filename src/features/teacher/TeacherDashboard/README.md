# Master Command Center: Teacher Dashboard

Welcome to the bridge, Sensei. 

This document serves as the human-readable manual for the **Teacher Dashboard Orchestrator**, the central nervous system of Nihongo Hub. This isn't just an interface; it's an immersive pedagogical environment where 3D atmosphere seamlessly melds with curriculum management.

## 🌌 The Vision
Our goal with the Teacher Dashboard is to entirely eliminate "digital fatigue." By replacing flat, sterile user interfaces with an expansive narrative environment, teachers are placed into a high-performance **Command Center**. Every interaction is designed to feel responsive, kinetic, and weightless.

## 🏗️ The Orchestrated Layout
The dashboard architecture is segmented to keep your workspace clear and frictionless:

1. **The Sidebar (Control Console):** Your primary navigation map. Retracts gracefully when not needed to maximize screen real estate.
2. **The TopNav (Heads Up Display):** Hosts your instant search metrics, Zen Mode (Dark/Light) toggle, and critical system notifications.
3. **The Dynamic Content Stage:** The central viewport. This massive stage automatically clears and mounts entirely new modules as you navigate, keeping the cognitive load minimal.

## 🪐 The Immersive Background (Antigravity Environment)
You will notice the background is not a static color wall. The **Antigravity 3D Kanji Environment** acts as a visual anchor. As you navigate from the "Resource Vault" to "Live Classrooms," the massive cinematic watermarks shift (from "資源" to "生"). This subtle, constant feedback grounds the user, mimicking the feeling of moving through physical sectors of a futuristic high-tech academy.

## ⚡ Core Navigation
Transitions between modes are functionally instantaneous and beautifully animated:
- **The Vault (Course Materials):** Manage batches and economics. 
- **Live Classrooms:** Trigger WebRTC broadcasts and manage active rosters.
- **Analytics & Dashboard Home:** Your zoomed-out view of student performance and overarching schedules.

Everything dissolves and slides into place effortlessly. Welcome aboard.
