# Teacher Batches Module: The Resource Vault

Welcome, Sensei.

This document serves as your guide to the **Teacher Batches** module—the foundational "Resource Vault" within Nihongo Hub. This module acts as your centralized command center for creating, scaling, and managing world-class Japanese learning products ranging from foundational N5 blocks to absolute mastery at N1.

## 🌌 The Vision
Our goal is to transcend simple course uploads. The Resource Vault empowers the Sensei to build dynamic ecosystems. You dictate the curriculum, the pricing models, and the exact collaborative team of co-teachers that will help you bring your course to life.

## 💎 The Antigravity Aesthetic
Your interaction with the vault is bound by the principles of **Antigravity Design**:
- **Hover Physics:** Interactive data cards lift and float responsively as you navigate your batches.
- **Dynamic 3D Canvases:** The massive Japanese Kanji backgrounds aren't static—they physically react to your navigational momentum and scroll depth, providing a cinematic, immersive 3D illusion without compromising layout speeds.

## 🚀 Key Feature Set
The modular wizard empowers deep control:
1. **Multi-Tiered Batch Creation:** Target specific JLPT proficiencies and construct focused learning environments.
2. **Premium vs. Free Models:** Protect your heavy-lifting courses behind payment gates or open basic foundations for free global access.
3. **Dynamic Coupon Generation:** Synthesize temporary or use-restricted promotional codes to boost your student rosters.
4. **The Collaborator Registry:** Scale your teaching presence by integrating specialized guest teachers into your courses.

## 🤝 Collaborative Synergy ("Lead Teacher" System)
Every batch relies on a strict hierarchical structure:
- **Lead Teacher (The Owner):** The Sensei who initially crafts the batch. Retains ultimate authority over curriculum data, pricing structures, and overall deletion. 
- **Invited Senseis (Collaborators):** Fellow platform educators brought in via direct User ID (UID). These co-hosts share the stage, gaining access to the live grids and batch resources, expanding the learning dynamics for the ultimate student experience.
